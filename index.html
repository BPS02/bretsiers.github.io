About

I'm Bret Siers — a creator, dreamer, and explorer of sound. I use artificial intelligence as my instrument to compose music that captures real human emotions. Every track is a story, every beat is intentional, and every melody is meant to move you. Whether it's the hope in a new beginning or the comfort of finding your way home, my music is about connection.

This journey started with a curiosity about what happens when technology meets feeling. What I found was something powerful — a new way to create, express, and share. I'm just getting started, and I'd love for you to be part of this ride.
