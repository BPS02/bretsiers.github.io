Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

Bret Siers Music ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you visit our website or use our app.

Information We Collect

Play counts: We store song play counts locally in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Email address: If you choose to join our mailing list, we collect your email address through Formspree to send occasional updates about new music releases. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party advertising. We do not collect personal information beyond what you voluntarily provide.

Third-Party Services

Formspree: Our mailing list form is powered by Formspree. When you submit your email, it is processed by Formspree under their privacy policy.

GitHub Pages: This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, browser type) as described in their privacy statement.

Data Storage

All user preferences and play data are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. No personal data is stored on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through Instagram.
